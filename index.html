Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our design services

Our project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of your design needs. For logo design, we typically require 2-3 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. Website design projects usually take 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive branding packages can span 6-12 weeks. We always provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and work closely with you to meet your specific deadlines. Rush services are available for urgent projects with additional fees.

Yes! We believe in getting your design exactly right. Our standard packages include unlimited revisions during the design phase to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. We'll keep refining the design based on your feedback until you're 100% happy. However, major scope changes or additional design directions beyond the original brief may incur additional fees. We discuss revision policies in detail during our initial consultation to ensure complete transparency.

We deliver all designs in multiple formats to ensure maximum versatility. Typical deliverables include high-resolution PNG and JPG files for web use, vector files (AI and EPS) for scalability, PDF files for printing, and source files for future modifications. For web projects, we provide optimized files for various devices and screen sizes. We also include brand guidelines documentation that specifies proper file usage, color codes, and technical specifications. Custom file formats can be provided upon request at no additional cost.

Our pricing is based on several factors including project scope, complexity, timeline, and your specific requirements. We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price packages for standard services, hourly rates for consulting work, and custom quotes for comprehensive projects. During our initial consultation, we discuss your budget, goals, and deliverables to create a transparent proposal with no hidden costs. We also offer payment plans for larger projects to make our services more accessible. We're committed to providing exceptional value and can work within various budget ranges.
